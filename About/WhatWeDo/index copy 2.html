About Metaverse Daily

We at Metaverse Daily, as a futuristic team building a platform for metaverse enthusiats and followers to stay updated across the industry, researches, events, and latest incomings. Platform facilitates virual universities and research labs on each segment of research area through dedicated channels.

Curates latest education programs i.e. certifications and courses and invites metaverse educaters to monetize their courses. Establishing partnerships with multiple metaverse business segment players to enable marketplace.

Additionally, what we do:

1. Broadcasting updates
2. Curated posts
3. Organizing labs
4. Hosting events
5. Education Programs
6. Promotions
7. Partnerships and marketplace
