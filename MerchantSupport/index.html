    Merchant Portal

    The Merchant Portal is your go-to hub for managing your account, processing transactions, tracking performance, and accessing resources to help you succeed. Through the portal, you can easily:

    1. Access Sales Analytics: Track your sales, revenue, and performance metrics.
    2. Manage Payments: View transaction history, process refunds, and manage payouts.
    3. Update Product Listings: Edit, upload, and manage your product catalog.
    4. Customer Support Tools: Access support resources to assist your customers.

    Access the Merchant Portal: Become Merchant and Login with provided credentials.
