    Information for Member Access

    All Metaverse Daily Members should have proper credentials shared officially. We have started accepting applications directly for memberships with references from atleast one current member.

    All applications, including references, are required to be submitted at memberships@metaversedaily.world or

    A complete application includes the following: 

    • Statement of objectives
    • Own Portfolio (Only Social Profiles from LinkedIn, Twitter & Mastodon)
    • Requested Membership
    • Reference (Referral Code) from Current Member.

    The Labs and Researches are supported by a diverse array of members, including some of from world's leading organizations. Our members do more than contribute to Digital Universities and Research Labs ongoing operating agenda; they also help us to conduct research in a unique, highly collaborative, interdisciplinary environment where academics, industry and organizations work together to find solutions and innovations for enabling metaverse ecosystem for larger base.

    The Member Portal provides advisory and research members to access exclusive content, various pro bono opportunities, decision making and events participations related to metaverse daily's initiatives. Signing into the Member Portal will also allow you to access information on the main research website and respective channels that the general public doesn't see; simply remain logged in when you browse the main site.
