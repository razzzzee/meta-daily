    How it Works

    • All Workshops are offered by Digital Universities or by Educators with association to Digital Universities.

    • All Workshops's credentials and reward points get stored in Blockchain and governed by Digital University DAO for forever verifiable and immutable. Indiviual's Web3 wallet would be source of authencity for their credentials.

    • There are respective difficulty level (Advanced, Intermediate, Beginner) for indiviual workshop with reward points associated as per level. Reward points governance done by Digital Univeristy DAO governing protocols.

    • Associated Pricing for each program whether from University or Educators is also governed by Digital University governing protocols.

    • There are two types of learning methodology for workshop

      1. Only Assignment based with reference materials
      2. Lecture and Assignment based with reference materials

    • 1 - Enroll for Offered Program
      First step is to keep enrolling for existing programs and upcoming one.
    • 2 - Review Process for Approval
      Our Digital University DAO governing the review process and approval for your profile for selected program.
    • 3 - Complete Program in Learning Center
      Once you get the approval, start with learning instructions provided in your Learning Center. You need to login with your provided EmailId.
    • 4 - Get Your Credits
      Start accumulating your individual program credits for bigger one i.e. undergrade or grade degree offered by Digital Universities. You can check you eligibilty on your profile.
    • 5 - Earn Additional Reward Points
      Each program has reward points on completion. Each as much as reward points by completing enrolled programs.
    • 6 - Get Eligible for Exclusive Offerings
      Your reward points makes you eligible for frequent NFT miniting and exclusive offerings from Digital Universities and Our Platform. Earning a specified count of reward points make your eligible for Digital University Council and for becoming an Educator.

    Digital University DAO (Decentralized Autonomous Organization) gives you an opportunity to become Digital Council Member to own governance authority and voting rights on unversity affairs and proposals to run organization in democratic way. Every council member own organization tokens and can grow their tokens by adding more education programs and by workshop of learners. Owning tokens make your vote weightage respective to your token count as each token gives to 1 vote right. Know More about how Digital University DAO work, Please visit Digital University section

    • 2 - Review Process for Approval
      Our Digital University DAO governing the review process and approval for your profile and quality of sample video.
    • 3 - Get Your First Program Listed
      Add your first program from educator board. You will receiving credentials for Educator Board after approval.
