How it Works

Digital University DAO (Decentralized Autonomous Organization) gives you an opportunity to become Digital Council Member to own governance authority and voting rights on unversity affairs and proposals to run organization in democratic way. Every council member own organization tokens and can grow their tokens by adding more education programs and by participation of learners. Owning tokens make your vote weightage respective to your token count as each token gives to 1 vote right. Know More about how Digital University DAO work, Please visit Digital University section

  • 1 - Apply for Educator
    First step is to apply for educator with us by filling an application with sample lecture video (max 5 mins) and profile. Your video should be hosted on public video stream services i.e. (Youtube, Vimeo etc.)
  • 2 - Review Process for Approval
    Our Digital University DAO governing the review process and approval for your profile and quality of sample video.
  • 3 - Get Your First Program Listed
    Add your first program from educator board. You will receiving credentials for Educator Board after approval.
  • 4 - Earn Reward Points
    Start accumulating your program by completion of programs by learners. Earn rewards qualify you for Council Member.
  • 5 - Promote Your Program
    Explore our various channels to engage with learners to promote your programs. Even our platform make your program reached to right learners. More learners get you more rewards points.
  • 6 - Get Eligible for Exclusive Offerings
    Your reward points makes you eligible for frequent NFT miniting and exclusive offerings from Digital Universities and Our Platform.
