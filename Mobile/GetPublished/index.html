How it Works

  • Publications and Participations are offered by Digital Universities or by Educators with association to Digital Universities.

  • Publications and Participations provide learners to collaborate with other learners along with educators to get more insights on forum purpose.

  • 1 - Get Columnist Membership
    First step is to apply for columnist membership for start writing.
  • 2 - Start Writing
    Explore challenges to participate directly to platform or in community run activities. Every particiation go through Hybrid AI review cycle to safeguarding community guidelines.
  • 3 - Score for Leaderboard
    For every publication challenge 100 score points rewared and 50 score points for participation challenge.
  • 4 - Earn Reward Points
    Start earning reward points for every participation. Our Built-in AI would assign points respective to your participation quality and your membership options.
  • 5 - Use Earned Points
    Earned points can be redeemed on official NFTs and programs run by Digital Universities. More you participate, more your get from our platform.
  • 6 - Get Eligible for NFT mints
    Your reward points makes you eligible for frequent NFT miniting from Digital Universities and Our Platform.
