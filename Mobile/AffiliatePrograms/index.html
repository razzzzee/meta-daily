6. Dedicated Affiliate Support: Our affiliate team is here to help you succeed with prompt support and guidance whenever you need it.

3. Promote Our Products: Share your affiliate link through various channels such as your blog, website, social media platforms, email newsletters, and more. We provide you with all the creative assets you need to make promoting easy.

Payment and Payouts

We believe in transparent and timely payouts. Here's how we handle payments for our affiliates:

1. Payment Frequency: Affiliates are paid monthly, with payments processed at the beginning of each month for the previous month's earnings.
2. Payment Methods: We offer several convenient payment options, including Crypto Wallet, PayPal, direct bank transfer, or other methods depending on your location.
3. Minimum Payout Threshold: The minimum payout amount is 200$. If your earnings don't meet the threshold in one payment cycle, they will roll over to the next month until you reach the minimum.
