Frequent Asked Questions?

How to get involved and get membership in research labs?
You have to select your area of interest and then accordingly apply for that specific research lab membership through our memberhip section. We have dedicated discord channels where you will be invited if got shortlisted.

What is difference between memberships and research labs?
Both are invitation only with different purpose where Memberships provide you access to be part of Metaverse Daily ongoing activities and research labs are open community based moderated channel to get involved in research areas.

How to establish a partnerships with Metaverse Daily?
We are still in ongoing process of enabling the business areas where partners can start sponsorships.

How to get my article published in daily broadcasts?
First you need to apply for Columnists membership through membership section. Once get selected, you can submit your publication to review and publish. You can start with reviewer membership first.

What is purpose of marketplace in Metaverse Daily?
Current portal act as gateway to metaverse world updates and researches where users would also be able to buy NFTs, Cryto and merchandise on same portal.
